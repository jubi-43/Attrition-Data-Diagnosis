# Attrition-Data-Analysis
Attritiion Data Analyis - Visual Rrepresentation using PowerBi

1. What is the overall health of the workforce?
   VISUAL:  KPI CARDS (Workforce size,Avg Monthly Salary,Attrition Count,Dominant Age Group)
   TAKEAWAYS: Attrition is numerically low, but occurring in a high-cost, experienced workforce.

2. Where is attrition concentrating?
   VISUALS : CLUSTERED COLUMN CHART - ATTRITION BY DEPARTMENT
   TAKEAWAYS: Sales requires role redesign or incentive review, not generic retention programs

3. At what career stage are people leaving?
   VISUALS: CLUSTERED BAR CHART  (Age Group)
   TAKEWAYS: Career progression & internal mobility gaps, not compensation alone.

4.Is attrition skewed by gender?
   VISUALS: TREEMAP (proportional attrition split between male and female employees)
   TAKEWAYS : Attrition is operationally driven, not diversity-risk driven. No alarming gender imbalance

5. Does education level influence attrition?
   VISUALS: DONUT CHARTS (attrition composition by education qualification)
   TAKEAWAYS: Upskilling & role enrichment matter more than pay hikes here
